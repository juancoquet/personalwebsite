# Aeroplanes & birds analogy for intelligence
In the same way that aeroplanes are able to fly faster than birds without needing to develop feathers, so too might intelligence evolve to be much more powerful than human intelligence without needing to develop consciousness. A different kind of intelligence might emerge, completely unlike our own.
